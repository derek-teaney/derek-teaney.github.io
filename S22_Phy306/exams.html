Basic content
  • Probability and probability distributions. Probability on a sphere. Random walk will not be on the exam.
  • The velocity distributions, flux through a hole, pressure, etc. Classical harmonic oscillator
  • Basic partition functions for two state systems, and harmonic oscillator.
  • The first law of thermodynamics, isothermal and adiabatic expansions, and engine cycles.
  • The second law of thermodynamics. Definition of entropy. Basica problems working with entropy
  • Estimates
